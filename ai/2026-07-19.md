## Counter-narrative

- **The "Anthropic price silence is a strategic mistake" narrative is over-fit on one product cycle.** Anthropic has held capability-defensive pricing through prior open-weight competitor waves (DeepSeek V3, Llama 4, Qwen 3.7) without repricing and lost less enterprise share than the token-price gap implied. Enterprise willingness-to-pay on frontier reasoning is empirically less price-elastic than commodity inference; Fable 5's Trust-and-Safety, red-team-hardened, and Constitution-tuned output remains materially more defensible in regulated deployment than a 2.8T-parameter model whose weights and safety scaffolding are still theoretical (weights ship July 27). A pricing response this week would concede a price war Anthropic can lose only on its own terms; holding the current pricing and letting the K3-weights independent-verification cycle play out is a defensible bet. The counter-narrative may be over-fitting to a 72-hour news cycle that will look different by end of July.

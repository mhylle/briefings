## AI & Software

**Anthropic tops the 2026 AI Safety Index — with a C+.** The Future of Life Institute's new index gave Anthropic the highest grade of any frontier lab; OpenAI and Google DeepMind landed at C, Meta at D+, and xAI, DeepSeek and Mistral effectively failed. Anthropic also reportedly hired Andrej Karpathy and Monzo co-founder Tom Blomfield. [ThursdAI](https://thursdai.news/releases/2026-07)

**Meta launches Muse Spark 1.1 and its first paid developer API.** Zuckerberg unveiled a 1M-token-context agentic model that Meta claims rivals GPT-5.5 and Opus 4.8 on agentic evals, shipping with computer use across desktop, browser and mobile and parallel subagent delegation. The developer API is in public preview (US-only, $20 free credits). [ThursdAI](https://thursdai.news/releases/2026-07)

**Google DeepMind delays Gemini 3.5 Pro to July 17 for a full rebuild.** Google scrapped the 2.5 Pro architecture entirely, targeting gains in mathematical reasoning, SVG scene generation and image quality to compete with GPT-5.6 and Anthropic's Fable 5. [BigGo Finance](https://finance.biggo.com/news/6f0c6bb2-795f-4c57-9d09-6db691d7638a)

**CISA flags an actively exploited flaw in Langflow.** An access-control bug (CVE-2026-55255) in the popular visual agent-building framework was added to CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog — an insecure direct object reference letting authenticated users invoke other users' flows. Worth checking if your team runs Langflow. [AI Agents News](https://aiagentstore.ai/ai-agent-news/this-week)

## Denmark

Specific models are classified; the Defence Ministry cites lessons from Ukraine. **Heatwave and drought prompt rare wildfire warnings.** Summer temperatures near 30°C led Danish authorities to warn of natural fires — uncommon in Denmark but capable of spreading fast — alongside warnings of water shortages. [DR](https://www.dr.dk/nyheder)

## World

**US strikes Iran for a fourth night; Iran hits back at Gulf bases.** US forces struck dozens of targets near the Strait of Hormuz and reinstated a blockade of Iranian ports; Iran said it targeted US infrastructure in Kuwait, Bahrain and Jordan. Iran's Health Ministry reports 260+ injured. Trump dropped a planned 20% Strait of Hormuz transit fee at Gulf allies' request. [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/15/world/live-news/iran-war-trump)

**Oil markets face the shock without their usual cushion.** The IMF notes strategic crude stockpiles that once buffered price spikes are depleted, with a ~4M barrel/day deficit between March and May covered by inventory drawdowns — leaving less slack as the Iran conflict escalates. [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/15/world/live-news/iran-war-trump)

**Ebola outbreak in eastern Congo outpaces the response.** WHO says 80% of new cases stem from unknown transmission chains; deaths have passed 700 (of ~1,926 infected) in the fastest-growing Ebola outbreak on the continent, driven by the rare Bundibugyo virus amid conflict and funding gaps. [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/07/15/g-s1-133630/ebola-congo-deaths) · [STAT](https://www.statnews.com/2026/07/14/ebola-outbreak-congo-new-cases-unknown-chains-of-transmission/)

**Wildfire smoke blankets the US Midwest and Northeast.** Heavy smoke from large wildfires in Canada and Minnesota is degrading air quality across broad swaths of the northern US. [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/wildfires-smoke-millions-exposed-midwest-northeast-us/)
